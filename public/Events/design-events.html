  • GLIMPSE INTO THE UNFOCUSED - Photography event

    We are challenging you to express opinions and voice it in the medium of photographs.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. This is an individual competition, allowing only one entry per individual.
    2. Image data file format : jpeg
    3. File size : not more than 4MB.
    4. A short description of 75 words or less about the photograph is mandatory.
    5. PROCESS : There are a total of two rounds - the first round and the final second round- after which the Top three winners and 10 special mentions will be announced.
    6. Submissions for round 1 - 3 weeks prior to the event date.
    7. Announcement of participants who made it to round 2 will be done two weeks prior to the event date.
    8. The shutter speed, ISO, Aperture, and camera make should be mentioned.
    9. Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
    10.PROCESS : Participants to take photo on any one of the themes given and write a short description about the same.
    11.SUBMISSION FORMAT : Submissions (JPEG) along with a short description of 75 words or less about the same to be uploaded in the submission form.

  • STEP-UP - Shoe Design Event


    This event aims to make the participants research on materials, as well as ergonomic requirements, to develop an innovative shoe design that fits the topic given.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. Participants can participate individually or in teams of 2.
    2. Submission requirements : sketches and a prototype (Digital renders or handmade).
    3. A short description of 75 words or less about the design is mandatory.
    4. PROCESS : Participants make sketches of the shoe design and a prototype to show the form of the designed shoe based on the theme provided.
    5. SUBMISSION FORMAT : Submission (PDF) titled STEP UP_TEAM NUMBER (example, team number 1 should submit as STEP UP_TEAM 1)

  • DESIGN CHARRETTE - Architectural Design Event

    As the world’s plastics take over Earth’s rivers and choke our ecosystems, can you find a way to take it back and transfigure plastic’s smothering nature into a serene floating meditation space, on one of the very rivers that it came from?

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1.Follow the design brief from the event website.
    2.Team size - 1-3 members.
    3.All text must be in English.
    4.The software used can be of one’s own choice.
    5.Participants can also incorporate walkthroughs and videos along with the deliverables specified in the design brief if they wish to.
    6.20 entries will be shortlisted for the jury presentation which will be held on 27th March 2021, on an online platform.
    7.Each shortlisted team will be given 15 mins to present their design scheme to the jury.
    8.Registration deadline- 20 March 2021.

  • REFRAME-INTO THE PAST - Illustrative Read Aloud Event

    Participants are provided with an interesting opportunity to consider a particular event in history and put forward a storyboard showcasing their ideas

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. 2 members in a group.
    2.Participants select a historical topic from the given options and create illustrations on how they would reimagine it to be, by altering the actual story.
    3.The illustrations can be shown as a set of storyboards or animation.
    4.Participants must present these illustrations along with any form of script (poem/speech) which will be read out during the presentation of the illustrations.
    5.The illustrations can be hand drawn or digitally made.
    6.The illustrations must not contain any form of text other than dialogues.
    7.A total of 5 minutes will be given for the presentation of the illustrations along with the narration.
    8.Offensive or sensitive content will not be qualified for the performance.
    9.Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
    10.Registration fee - Rs. 500/-
